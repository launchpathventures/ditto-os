Subcontractor Agreement

Based on NZS 3910:2013 Conditions of Contract

1. Parties

Principal:R.M. Building Ltd, 18 Colombo Street, Christchurch 8011
Subcontractor:David Kearney T/A Kearney Foundations, 7 Marshland Road, Christchurch 8083

2. Project

2.1 The Works comprise foundation and concrete slab construction at 42 Rimu Street, Halswell, Christchurch 7674 ("the Site"), as detailed in the attached scope of works (Schedule A).
2.2 The Subcontractor shall execute and complete the Works in accordance with the drawings, specifications, and conditions set out in this Agreement.

3. Contract Sum

3.1 The Contract Sum is $38,500.00 (plus GST) as a fixed lump sum price, inclusive of all labour, materials, plant, and equipment necessary to complete the Works.
3.2 Payment shall be made monthly in arrears upon receipt of a valid tax invoice, subject to a retention of 5% up to a maximum of $1,925.00.

7. Insurance

7.1 The Subcontractor shall maintain the following insurance cover for the duration of the Works:
Insurance TypeMinimum CoverEvidence Required
Public Liability$2,000,000Certificate of currency
Professional Indemnity$1,000,000Certificate of currency
Motor Vehicle — Third Party$1,000,000Policy schedule
Workers' CompensationStatutoryACC cover letter
7.2 Certificates of currency must be provided to the Principal prior to commencing work on Site and upon each renewal during the contract period.

8. Health & Safety

8.1 The Subcontractor acknowledges their duties as a Person Conducting a Business or Undertaking (PCBU) under the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015 (HSWA).
8.2 The Subcontractor shall:
(a) provide a site-specific safety plan prior to commencing works;
(b) ensure all workers hold current Site Safe certification;
(c) comply with the Principal's site safety rules and induction requirements;
(d) report all notifiable events to the Principal within 2 hours of occurrence.
8.3 The Principal reserves the right to suspend the Works immediately if, in their reasonable opinion, the Subcontractor is in breach of any health and safety obligation.

4. Programme

4.1 The Subcontractor shall commence the Works on or about 17 March 2026, subject to reasonable notice from the Principal and site readiness.
4.2 The Works shall be completed by 28 April 2026⚑ Confirm with Dave, being six (6) weeks from the commencement date.
4.3 If the Subcontractor fails to complete the Works by the date for completion, liquidated damages of $250.00 per working day shall apply unless an extension of time has been granted in writing.

5. Variations

5.1 No variation to the Works shall be carried out unless authorised in writing by the Principal or the Principal's representative.
5.2 Variations shall be valued at fair and reasonable rates. Where possible, rates shall be derived from the rates and prices in this Agreement.

6. Defects Liability

6.1 The defects liability period shall be twelve (12) months from the date of practical completion.
6.2 During the defects liability period, the Subcontractor shall rectify any defects notified by the Principal at no additional cost, within a reasonable time having regard to the nature and extent of the defect.
6.3 Retention monies shall be released as follows: 50% upon practical completion; 50% upon expiry of the defects liability period, subject to all defects being rectified.
