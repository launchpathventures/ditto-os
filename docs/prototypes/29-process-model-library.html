Invoice follow-up
Chase overdue invoices automatically. Sends reminders at 7, 14, and 30 days. You review escalations only.
How it works
1. Check for invoices overdue by 7, 14, or 30 days
2. Draft a reminder email matching the overdue period
3. Send reminder (7-day and 14-day: automatic after trust earned)
4. Flag 30-day overdue for your review — these may need a phone call
5. If payment received, close and update records
Apps needed
Sending reminders → Gmail (detected)
Checking invoices → Xero (connected)
Quality standards included
Follows up at 7, 14, 30 days · Escalates unpaid after 30 days · Never sends on weekends · Professional but firm tone · Includes invoice number and amount in every reminder
What you'll review
30-day escalation decisions · Template changes · Any invoice over $5,000
