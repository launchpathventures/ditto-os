Hi. I'm Ditto.

I'm not a chatbot. I'm a working partner that learns your business, remembers everything, and gets better over time. Let me show you how I'm different — and get to know you.

What makes this different from ChatGPT or Claude
  • I remember everything. Our conversations build knowledge that compounds. I never start from scratch.
  • I do the work, not just answer questions. I write your content, run your routines, and learn from your corrections.
  • I earn your trust. You start by checking everything. Over time, as I prove reliable, I handle more on my own.
  • I explain my thinking. Every output shows what I used and why. You can always see under the hood.
Let's get started
Takes about 10 minutes. I'll learn who you are, what you do, and how I can help.
