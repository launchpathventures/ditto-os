# Insight-145: Relationship-First Beats Volume — Market Data Confirms

**Date:** 2026-04-05
**Trigger:** AI SDR market failure analysis — 50-70% churn, hybrid model data
**Layers affected:** L1 Process, L3 Harness, L5 Learning
**Status:** active

## The Insight

Market data from the AI SDR correction of 2025-2026 overwhelmingly validates Ditto's relationship-first model:

- AI-only outreach: 847 meetings, 11% opportunity conversion
- AI + human hybrid: 312 meetings, 38% opportunity conversion, 2.3x more revenue
- Warm introductions: 3% no-show (vs 25% cold), 3-5x conversion rate
- AI SDR platforms: 50-70% churn within 3 months

The entire AI SDR market optimised for volume and is now experiencing a correction. The tools that work use AI for research and personalization, with humans for judgment and relationships. Ditto's model — low volume, deep personalization, supervised trust progression, relationship memory — is exactly what the market data says should work.

This is not a contrarian bet. This is what the data shows after the market ran the volume experiment and it failed.

## Implications

1. Ditto's trust tier model (supervised → spot-checked → autonomous) directly maps to what the hybrid model literature recommends: "Start with human-in-the-loop, earn trust through quality, never fully automate judgment."
2. The right KPI is revenue-per-introduction, not meetings-booked. Process metrics should track conversion quality, not volume.
3. "5 perfect emails/week" is not a limitation — it's the strategy. Ditto should resist any pressure to increase volume as a growth lever.
4. The 15-20 hours/week human oversight finding translates to: supervised tier needs to feel lightweight, not burdensome.
