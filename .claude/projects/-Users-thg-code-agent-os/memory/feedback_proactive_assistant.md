name: Self must be proactively managing attention
description: The Self should proactively manage focus, attention, opportunities, coverage gaps, and upcoming work — like a great executive assistant, not a passive tool
type: feedback
---

The Self must be proactive about five things: focus (what matters most right now and why), attention (things slipping off the radar), opportunities (patterns and connections the user might miss), coverage (gaps in the operation), and upcoming work (anticipating before it's urgent).

**Why:** A great personal assistant doesn't wait to be asked — they notice things and mention them at the right moment. Our personas' core anxiety is "things falling through the cracks." The Self eliminates that by actively managing the user's attention across their whole operation.

**How to apply:** Every design involving the Self's behaviour should include proactive dimensions. The morning briefing is the primary vehicle — all five dimensions weave naturally into the narrative. Beyond briefing: 1-2 proactive nudges per session. Never noisy, always offered as a colleague would. Insight-076 captures the full model.
